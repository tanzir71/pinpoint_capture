PinPoint Capture

Minimal, precise screen recordings

Lightweight screen recorder with click‑focused smart zoom. Capture your screen and optional microphone audio, and save precise MP4 recordings — ideal for tutorials, bug reports, and handoffs.

View on GitHub Download Release

How it works: PinPoint listens for your mouse clicks and automatically zooms into the clicked region to spotlight the action. After a short, configurable duration, it smoothly zooms back out to your baseline view. You control the zoom level, zoom-in duration, and transition speed to match your demo style.
